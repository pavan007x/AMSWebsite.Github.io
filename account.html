Account Management

  • Creation of account heads in the system
  • Direct link of Receipts (student fee payments, hostel fee payments, fines and penalties) and Payments (salary payments, government fee payments, purchases and day-to-day expenses) to the account heads
  • Generation of receipts and expense vouchers
  • Generation of accounting reports such as Trial Balance, Profit and Loss and Balance Sheet
  • Leave management system for the staff to apply credit leaves, approve or reject the leaves
  • Staff Payroll calculation, pay slip generation and pay slip emails
